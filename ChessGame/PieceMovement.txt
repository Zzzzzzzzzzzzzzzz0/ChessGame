Pawn
-Moves forward but attacks diagonally.
-On its first move, a pawn has the option to advance two squares.
-Promotes to any other piece when it reaches the eighth rank.
Rook
-Moves horizontally or vertically any number of squares.
-Cannot jump over other pieces.
Knight
-Moves in an "L" shape: two squares in one direction and one square perpendicular to it.
-Can jump over other pieces.
Bishop
-Moves diagonally any number of squares.
-Cannot jump over other pieces.
Queen
-Combines the moves of a rook and a bishop.
-Moves horizontally, vertically, or diagonally any number of squares.
King
-Moves one square in any direction.
-Important piece to protect; if it's in a position to be captured (check), the player must make a move to avoid checkmate.
